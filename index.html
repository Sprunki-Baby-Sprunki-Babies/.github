About Sprunki Babies

Sprunki Babies is a creative music game where players combine sounds using baby-themed characters to create unique mixes. Inspired by Incredibox, this fan-made mod offers a lighthearted, accessible way to explore music.

How to Play

  • Drag and drop characters to create custom sound mixes.
  • Each character has unique sounds, like beats, melodies, and effects.
  • Experiment with combinations to unlock special interactions and animations.

Join the Community

Share your creations, get feedback, and connect with other players to enjoy the full experience of Sprunki Babies.
